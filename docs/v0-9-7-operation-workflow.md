# Version 0.9.7 — Operation Workflow

## Galaxy and Operations

- Planet operation entries are clickable.
- Clicking an operation in the Planet Inspector opens the Operations page,
  filtered to that planet and focused on that operation.
- Operation cards are color-coded by priority.
- Director operation cards expose Prepare Region and Deploy Players actions.

## Resolved Operations

- Added outcome filters for victories, draws, and defeats.
- Added resolution timestamps.
- Influence effects are displayed with faction-specific colors and names.

## Director workflow

A GM can now:

1. Select an operation from the galaxy or Operations page.
2. Prepare its configured region.
3. Deploy players.
4. Start the encounter.
5. Resolve the deployment through the Director dashboard.

The active AI battles continue progressing independently.
