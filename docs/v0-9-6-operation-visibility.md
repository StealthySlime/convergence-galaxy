# Version 0.9.6 — Operation Visibility and Archive

## Galaxy map

Planets with unresolved operations now display a numbered operation badge.

Selecting a planet displays its current available, active, or
awaiting-resolution operations in the Planet Inspector.

## Active operations

The Operations tab and Director operation management now display only
unresolved operations.

Resolved and cancelled operations cannot be deployed or extended.

## Resolved Operations

A new `Resolved Operations` tab archives completed operations and displays:

- planet
- outcome
- mission type
- difficulty
- stability effect
- friendly and enemy influence effects
- GM resolution notes

The old placeholder Events tab has been removed.
